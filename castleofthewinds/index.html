Castle of the Winds

Classic RPG, Reimagined

The beloved 1990s dungeon crawler rebuilt from scratch for modern Apple devices. Explore procedurally generated dungeons, battle mythical creatures, discover legendary loot, and avenge your family. A faithful reimagining that respects the original while embracing the future.

Adventure Awaits

Procedural Dungeons

Every playthrough is unique. Explore randomly generated dungeons filled with secrets, traps, and treasure.

Norse Mythology

Battle creatures from Norse legend. Giants, trolls, dragons, and more stand between you and vengeance.

Legendary Loot

Discover powerful weapons, armor, and magical artifacts. Identify mysterious items and grow stronger.

Turn-Based Combat

Think tactically. Every move matters in this classic roguelike combat system.

Magic System

Learn and cast spells from multiple schools of magic. Become a warrior-mage or pure spellcaster.

Cross-Device Saves

Start on iPhone, continue on iPad, finish on Mac. Your adventure syncs across all devices.
