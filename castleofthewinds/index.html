New to Castle of the Winds?

Castle of the Winds is a classic roguelike RPG where you descend into procedurally generated dungeons to avenge your family and reclaim your birthright.

What makes it special:

  • Permadeath: When you die, you start over. Every decision matters.
  • Procedural generation: Dungeons, loot, and encounters are randomized. No two playthroughs are the same.
  • Turn-based combat: Think tactically. Position matters. Gear matters. Strategy matters.
  • Norse mythology: Face giants, trolls, and dragons from ancient legend.

What makes this version special:

  • Touch-optimized controls for iPhone and iPad
  • Mouse/keyboard support for Mac
  • Modern graphics that honor the original aesthetic
  • iCloud sync across all your Apple devices
  • No ads, no in-app purchases, no nonsense

Questions?

Is this the original game?

No. This is a complete rebuild from scratch, faithfully recreating the gameplay, mechanics, and spirit of the original while modernizing the interface and platform.

Do I need to have played the original?

Not at all. If you love roguelikes or dungeon crawlers, you'll love this. The nostalgia is a bonus, not a requirement.

Is it really permadeath?

Yes. Classic roguelike rules apply. When you die, you start over. We do have difficulty settings if you want to ease into it.

How long is the game?

A single playthrough can take 10-20 hours, but the procedural generation and replayability mean you can play for hundreds of hours.

Are there in-app purchases?

No. Zero. Everything is included in the purchase price. No energy bars, no loot boxes, no premium currency.

Will there be updates?

Yes. We'll continue improving the game based on player feedback, all included in your original purchase.
