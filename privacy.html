Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Our Commitment

HeiloProjects is committed to protecting your privacy. We believe your data belongs to you, and we've built our apps with that principle at their core.

What We Don't Collect

Unlike many app developers, we take a privacy-first approach:

  • No personal data collection — We don't collect your name, email, or any identifying information through our apps
  • No tracking — We don't use analytics SDKs, tracking pixels, or any form of user tracking
  • No advertising — We don't show ads and therefore don't need to collect data to serve them
  • No third-party sharing — We don't share any data because we don't collect any

Your Data Stays Local

All data you create in our apps stays on your device or in your personal iCloud account. We cannot access your data, and we have no interest in doing so.

iCloud Sync

Some of our apps offer iCloud sync to keep your data in sync across your Apple devices. This data is stored in your personal iCloud account, managed by Apple according to their privacy policy. We have no access to your iCloud data.

Contact Forms

If you contact us through our website or email:

  • We collect only the information you provide (name, email, message)
  • We use this information solely to respond to your inquiry
  • We don't add you to any marketing lists
  • We delete correspondence after the inquiry is resolved

Website Analytics

Our website uses no third-party analytics or tracking services. We may review basic server logs to ensure the site is working properly, but these don't contain personally identifiable information.

Children's Privacy

Our apps do not collect any personal information, making them safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we'll post the new version here with an updated date. We'll never change our commitment to your privacy.

Questions?

If you have questions about our privacy practices, please contact us at heiloprojects@icloud.com.
