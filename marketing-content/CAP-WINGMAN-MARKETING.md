## FAQ Copy

**Q: Is this official CAP software?**
A: No. CAP Wingman is an unofficial app made by CAP members for CAP members. It's not affiliated with CAP National HQ.

**Q: Are the forms and regulations current?**
A: We update content regularly, but you should always verify critical information with official CAP sources or your chain of command.

**Q: Does it work completely offline?**
A: Yes. Download all content while you have internet, and everything works without connectivity.

**Q: Can I submit forms directly through the app?**
A: You can fill and export forms to PDF. Submission depends on your wing's process‚Äîemail, eServices, or printed copies.

**Q: How much storage does it use?**
A: Approximately 200-500 MB depending on how many documents you download. You can manage downloads to save space.

**Q: Is my training data shared with CAP?**
A: No. Your training tracker is private and stored only on your devices and iCloud. We don't share data with anyone.
