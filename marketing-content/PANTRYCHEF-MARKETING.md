## Content Tone Guidelines

**Voice Attributes:**
- Practical, not preachy
- Empowering, not guilt-inducing
- Warm, not corporate
- Solutions-focused, not problem-obsessed

**Language Patterns:**
- Use "you" and "your" frequently (personal)
- Use present tense ("discover," "track," "plan")
- Use active voice ("PantryChef shows you" not "You are shown")
- Avoid food waste guilt ("stop throwing away" vs. "reduce your environmental impact")

**Words to Use:**
- Know, discover, plan, organize, use, save, simple, smart, quick

**Words to Avoid:**
- Revolutionary, amazing, incredible, best, must-have (overpromising)
- Guilt, waste, bad, wrong (negative framing)
- Complex, sophisticated, advanced (intimidating)
