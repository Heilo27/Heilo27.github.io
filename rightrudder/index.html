The CFI's Dilemma

You didn't get your CFI to fill out forms. You got it to share your passion for flight.

But between tracking student progress, generating endorsements, filling out 8710s, scheduling lessons, and invoicing, the paperwork piles up. Spreadsheets become overwhelming. Details slip through the cracks.

Right Rudder was built by a CFI who lived this. It handles the admin so you can focus on teaching.

What You Can Do

Every Student, One View

Track progress against ACS standards. See who's ready for stage checks, who needs more work, and who's approaching checkride. View training history, hours logged, and next lesson plans. Know exactly where every student stands. Always.

Every Endorsement, Perfectly Formatted

All FAR Part 61 endorsements at your fingertips. Select a student, choose the endorsement, and it's formatted correctly with your CFI number, date, and signature. Export to PDF or print directly to student logbooks. Stop looking up regulation numbers. Stop retyping the same text.

Visual Progress Against ACS

See which maneuvers students have mastered and which need more work. Track ground lessons, flight lessons, and stage checks. Flag weak areas automatically. Generate progress reports for students and parents. No more guessing what to cover next.

Schedule Lessons, Track Hours, Get Paid

Calendar integration for lesson scheduling. Automatically log instructor hours. Generate invoices with itemized breakdowns—ground time, flight time, aircraft rental. Export for your accountant or flight school billing system. Stop losing track of billable hours.

Generate FAA Paperwork in Seconds

8710 forms, solo endorsements, recommendations for checkrides—all auto-populated from student records. No more manual form filling. No more typos. Just accurate, compliant documents. Submit checkride paperwork without the headache.

At the Airport, In Flight, In the Office

Record lesson notes in the cockpit without cell service. Update endorsements between flights. Everything syncs when you're back online. Use on iPhone at the airport, iPad in the briefing room, Mac in the office. Your flight school follows you. Everywhere.

Student Companion App Included

Your purchase includes the Right Rudder Student app. Students can view their progress, see upcoming lessons, access their endorsements, and stay on track—all synced with your instructor app. Keep students engaged between lessons.

Questions?

Is this approved by the FAA?

Right Rudder helps you generate compliant endorsements and forms, but you're still responsible for accuracy and signing. It's a tool, not a replacement for your CFI judgment.

What happens if I stop using the app?

Export all your data anytime. Student records, endorsements, billing history—it's all yours. No lock-in.

Does it work offline?

Yes. Record lesson notes at the airport without cell service. Everything syncs when you're back online.

Can my flight school use this?

Right Rudder is designed for individual CFIs. If you're a school owner looking to equip multiple instructors, contact us about volume licensing.

Can I import students from another system?

You can manually add students or import via CSV. We're working on direct integrations with popular flight school software.

What about CFI renewals?

Right Rudder tracks your students and their endorsements. For your personal CFI renewal, check with your FIRC provider or DPE.
