The Problem with Most Recipe Apps

They show you what you could make if you went shopping. But what about right now? What about the chicken in your fridge that expires tomorrow? What about using the canned tomatoes you bought three months ago?

PantryChef starts with what you have, not what you need to buy.

What You Can Do

Know What You Have

Scan barcodes, snap photos, or type it in. Track expiration dates so nothing goes bad before you use it. Organize by location—pantry, fridge, freezer—and see everything at a glance. Never buy duplicates. Never waste food.

Cook What You Own

Find recipes you can make right now. Not "almost"—actually make, with ingredients you have. Filter by meal type, diet, or cooking time. Works offline with 500+ built-in recipes. Stop buying ingredients for one recipe. Start using what's already there.

Plan Your Week in Minutes

Drag recipes onto your calendar. See what you can make, what you need to buy, and what needs using soon. Your meal plan stays flexible—swap meals, move them around, or skip a day. Know what's for dinner. Every night.

Buy Only What You Need

Add a recipe, get the missing ingredients instantly. Check off items as you shop. Share your list with family so anyone can grab groceries. Smaller grocery bills. Shorter shopping trips.

Add Ingredients Your Way

"Hey Siri, add milk to PantryChef." Snap a photo of your pantry shelf and let the app identify items. Type it in manually. However you work, PantryChef keeps up. Tracking your pantry shouldn't feel like work.

Your Kitchen, Your Devices, Your Data

Use on iPhone while shopping, iPad while cooking, Mac while planning. Everything syncs via iCloud. Your recipes and pantry stay private—no tracking, no ads, no data collection. One purchase. All your devices. Complete privacy.

Questions?

Is this a subscription?

Nope. Buy once, own forever. One price gets you everything, on all your Apple devices.

Does it work offline?

Yes. Core features and 500+ built-in recipes work without internet. Perfect for kitchens with spotty wifi.

Can I share with family?

Absolutely. Use Family Sharing to give your household access. Share shopping lists too.

Where does my data go?

Nowhere. It lives on your devices and syncs via your personal iCloud. We never see it.

What about dietary restrictions?

Filter recipes by vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, dairy-free, and more.

Can I add my own recipes?

Yes. Add custom recipes and PantryChef will track whether you have the ingredients.
